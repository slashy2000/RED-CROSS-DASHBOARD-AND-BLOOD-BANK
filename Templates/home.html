Mission

To save lives by providing safe blood and blood products in a cost-effective manner utilizing quality management systems as a fulfillment of the social responsibility of the Philippine Red Cross, a humanitarian and voluntary service organization.

Vision

Philippine Red Cross, the leading provider of safe and quality blood and blood products in the Philippines.
